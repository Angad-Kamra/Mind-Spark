Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join the community?

You can join our community by clicking the "Join Now" button and filling out the registration form. It's completely free!

What makes this community ADHD-friendly?

Our community features include: flexible communication styles, no-pressure participation, ADHD-friendly event scheduling, content warnings for overstimulation, and dedicated focus channels.

Are there age restrictions for joining?

We welcome members aged 13+, but require parental consent for users under 16. We have separate channels for different age groups to maintain appropriate content standards.

How do I find gaming buddies with similar interests?

Use our #matchmaking channel to fill out a quick interest form, or join our weekly "Game Buddy Speed Dating" events every Friday at 7PM UTC.

What safety measures are in place?

We employ: 24/7 moderation, content filtering, anonymous reporting, two-factor authentication, and regular community guideline reminders.

Can I organize my own event?

Absolutely! Use our event proposal form (found in #community-events) and our team will help with scheduling, promotion, and setup.

How do you handle sensory overload concerns?

We provide: optional simplified UI themes, audio-visual content warnings, text-only chat alternatives, and scheduled "quiet hours" in voice channels.
