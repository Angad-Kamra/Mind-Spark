What is ADHD?

ADHD (Attention-Deficit/Hyperactivity Disorder) is a neurodevelopmental condition characterized by persistent patterns of inattention, hyperactivity, and impulsivity. It affects both children and adults, influencing daily functioning and quality of life.

Common Symptoms

Inattention

Difficulty focusing, forgetfulness, and being easily distracted.

Hyperactivity

Restlessness, constant movement, or talking excessively.

Impulsivity

Interrupting others, acting without thinking, or impatience.

Coping Strategies

Routine Planning

Use daily planners or apps to structure your day and set reminders.

Mindfulness & Meditation

Regular mindfulness practice helps improve focus and reduce stress.

Exercise Regularly

Physical activity increases dopamine levels and enhances attention.

Helpful Tools & Apps

Todoist / Notion

Great for task management and building structured to-do lists.

Headspace / Calm

Apps for meditation and stress relief, aiding emotional regulation.

Focus To-Do / Pomodoro

Time management tools using Pomodoro technique for productivity.

Diet & Lifestyle

A balanced diet plays a key role in managing ADHD symptoms:

  • Include omega-3 rich foods like fish or flaxseed
  • Limit sugar and processed foods
  • Ensure proper hydration and sleep

Professional Help

If you or someone you know struggles with ADHD, consult a mental health professional for diagnosis and treatment. Therapy, coaching, or medication can significantly improve daily functioning.
